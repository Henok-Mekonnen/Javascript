Short version of my life story

My name is Henok Mekonnen I was born and raised in Addis Ababa, the capital city of Ethiopia, also the diplomatic center of Africa. Before I move to United States in January 2015, I was living with my mother, three of my brothers, two of my sisters, two of my nephews and one of my nieces. my father passed away when I was 12 years old, he was one of the well reputed civil case lawyers in the city and was the bread maker for the family. after finishing my high school, beside doing my college degree I was handling some family responsibilities on helping my mother.

After graduating from law school, I was practicing in the federal supreme court of Ethiopia as a clerk and case briefer for assistant judge. Even if I was not happy with the situation of the work and the payment, stayed until the end of the contract hoping to get some experience to practice as an independent lawyer.
